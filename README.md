# 🛒 Swiggy Instamart — Power BI Sales Analytics Dashboard

> An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyse grocery sales performance, order trends, and operational metrics across shop types, city tiers, and product categories.

---

## 📌 Short Description

The **Swiggy Instamart Power BI Dashboard** is a multi-page business intelligence report that transforms raw grocery shop transactional data into actionable insights. It enables stakeholders to monitor total sales, track order volumes, evaluate shop performance by city type, and identify top-selling categories — all through dynamic visuals and interactive slicers.

## ✨ Features & Highlights

### ⚠️ Business Problem

Grocery delivery platforms like Instamart operate across hundreds of shop locations in multiple city tiers, generating large volumes of sales and operational data. Key challenges include:
- Lack of visibility into which city types and shop sizes drive the most revenue
- Difficulty identifying top-performing product categories across locations
- No consolidated view of shop performance metrics like average sales and ratings
- Inability to track how shop openings have grown over the years

---

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To build a **self-service, interactive Power BI report** that allows business analysts, operations managers, and category teams to:
- Monitor core KPIs — Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Sales, and Average Rating — at a glance
- Compare performance across city types (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3) and shop sizes
- Identify best-selling grocery categories to drive inventory and promotional decisions
- Analyse shop opening trends over time to support expansion planning

### 💡 Business Impact & Insights

- 🏙️ **City Tier Analysis** — Column and donut charts reveal which city tier contributes most to total sales and orders, helping prioritise expansion and marketing spend
- 🏪 **Shop Size Performance** — Donut chart shows how small, medium, and high-size shops compare in revenue contribution
- 🛍️ **Category Strategy** — Bar chart and funnel identify top and bottom performing grocery categories, supporting inventory and promotional planning
- 📅 **Growth Tracking** — Line chart tracks the number of shops opened year-over-year, providing a clear picture of business expansion trends
- ⭐ **Quality Monitoring** — Average Rating KPI helps monitor customer satisfaction across shop types
- 📋 **Operational Summary** — The detail table gives a quick shop-type level view of all key metrics in one place
